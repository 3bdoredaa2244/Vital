HbA1c
Review · 5.8 %
Reflects your average blood sugar over the past 2–3 months — a stable, long-term view of glucose control that doesn't swing with your last meal.
Why it matters?
Elevated HbA1c is associated with prediabetes and type-2 diabetes risk. Keeping it in the optimal band reflects steady, well-controlled metabolic health.
